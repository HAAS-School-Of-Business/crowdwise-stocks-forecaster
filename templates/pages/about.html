How It Works

Submit Your Vote

Submit your vote for the live questions. Upon submission, you will gain access to the crowd's wisdom.

Build your Accuracy Score

When voting ends, a result will be finalized. Your accuracy score will be updated accordingly.

Impact the Wisdom of the Crowd

Directly impact the wisdom of the crowd. Your accuracy score will determine your vote's weight and directly influence the wisdom of the crowd.

Roadmap

Crowd Predictive Analytics (CPA) is currently in beta testing stages. The first 1000 active users of the platform during beta testing phase will be awarded our exclusive NFT which grants access to the rewards generated from the wisdom of the crowd.

NFT Mint and Air Drop

As an early active user of CPA's platform you are guaranteed an exclusive NFT which will grant you access to the financial rewards generated from the wisdom of the crowd. Mint & Air Drop date coming soon.

Crowd Builds Accuracy

Active users will build the crowd's accuracy score while building their own personal accuracy score.

Financial Rewards

When the crowd passes an accuracy threshold (yet to be determined), all 1000 active users will be compensated for their accuracy on a continuous basis.
