HOW DOES CROWD PREDICTIVE ANALYTICS WORK?

Crowd Predictive Analytics will generate questions for users to submit their predictions on. Users will have one chance to submit their prediction and will not be allowed to make any updates once their prediction has been submitted. Each question will have a  deadline and results will be posted once verified by CPA admin. When results have been posted, ever user's accuracy score will be updated.

Predictions are used as input for CPA's proprietary weighted formula's which calculate the crowd's prediction. Active users will gain access to the crowd's weighted prediction only after they have submitted their prediction for that question. The more predictions that our community submits, the higher the benefit for everyone in the network.

If the crowd accurately predicts the question(s) being asked, the users who participated will then be rewarded ONE CPA token. The more tokens a user holds, the greater the user's payout will be. During the BETA testing phases, token amounts will not appear on the user's dashboard, however, early registration users are receiving 2x tokens which is being recorded privately by CPA. Payout amounts are variable and depend on the nature of the question being asked. Users will be notified of payout policies as they continue to evolve.
